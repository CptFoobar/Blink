Introduction
Hi and welcome to Blink. You've probably come here to get things done fast, so we'll just get to the point and walk through main things you might want to do in Blink.
Content

The Content section allows you to manage your feed sources and choose where you feed comes from. From here you can select your favorite rss feeds and blogs indexed by Feedly
You can find it under the Options menu () on the far right of the nav bar.

Add Content Sources
You can add content to your feed with the following steps:
  1. Click the Add Content button () on the bottom-right of the Content page.
  2. In the dialog that opens, type in the feed you want to search for. A number of suggestions will be displayed
  3. When you find something you like, just click it or hit enter after selecting it.

Remove Content sources
If you no longer to wish to see content from a particular feed source, you can remove it from Blink with these steps:
  1. First, locate the feed source you want to remove in the content list.
  2. Click the small Delete button () on the top-right of the source's icon.
  3. A confirmation modal will pop up. If you are sure you want to delete the source, click the delete button. Else, you can click the cancel button to cancel the operation.
If you accidentally delete a source, you can always get it back using the Add Content button.
Settings


Settings section allow you to configure some general settings of the extension.
You can find it under the Options menu () on the far right of the nav bar.

Changing Username

To change the User name shown in the Greeting message, change the Greeting Name field in the Settings section.

Disable Home screen greeting

You can also disable the greeting message on the home screen by unchecking the 'Show Greeting' option in the settings.

Feed content ratio
Blink gives you the option to choose the type of feed you want to see. You can choose from three modes, Latest, Balanced and Trending.
  • Latest populates your feed with the most recent items from your sources.
  • Trending shows you only the hot topics of the day, so you can quickly catch up on the important headlines.
  • Balanced gives you a nice blend of latest and trending news from your feed sources.

    • Don't forget to save your settings by clicking the Save button (   ) on the bottom-right.
