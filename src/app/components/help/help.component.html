Introduction
Hi and welcome to Blink. Blinks a clean and friendly New Tab replacement that brings your favorite feeds from all over the iternet right into your new tab page. Let's take a look at what you can do with it!
Feed

The Feed section is where your news feed lives.
You can reach it by clicking the icon on the top right of the screen.


Content

The Content section allows you to manage your feed sources. From here you can select your favorite feeds and blogs indexed by Feedly.
You can find it under the Options menu () on the top right of the screen.

Add Content Sources
You can add content to your feed with the following steps:
  1. Click the Add Content button () on the bottom-right of the Content page.
  2. In the dialog that opens, type in the feed you want to search for.
    You can search specific sources (like TechCrunch, HuffPost) or general topics (Politics, Science).
  3. When you find something you like, just click it or hit enter after selecting it.

Remove Content sources
If you no longer wish to see content from a particular feed source, you can remove it from Blink with these steps:
  1. First, locate the feed source you want to remove in the content list.
  2. Click the small Delete button () on the top-right of the source's icon.
If you accidentally delete a source, you can always get it back using the Add Content button.

Disable Content sources
If you wish to temporarily disable feed from a particular source, without actually removing it from your list, here's what you can do:
  1. locate the feed source you want to disable in the content list.
  2. Uncheck the little checkbox () present before the source's name.
The feed will now remain hidden until you check this box again.

Settings


Settings section allow you to configure some general settings of the extension.
You can find it under the Options menu () on the top right of the screen.

Change Username

You can customize the name shown in the Greeting message by changing the "Greet by name" setting.

Disable Home screen greeting

You can also disable the greeting message on the home screen by unchecking the 'Show Greeting' option in the settings.

Shuffling the feed

If you dont' want Blink to shuffle the news items on your feed page, uncheck the "Shuffle Feed" option.

Feed content ratio
Blink gives you the option to choose the type of feed you want to see. You can choose one out of the three types, Latest, Balanced and Trending.
  • Latest populates your feed with the freshest content from your sources.
  • Trending shows you only the hot topics of the day, so you can quickly catch up on the important headlines.
  • Balanced gives you a nice blend of latest and trending news from your feed sources.

    • Change Feed view
    Blink offers you the option to change how your news feed looks. You can choose from two modes: Cozy and Compact

    Don't forget to save your settings by clicking the Save button () on the bottom-right.
